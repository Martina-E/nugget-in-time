The War at a Glance

The War was one that was defined by several significant events, in both the East and the West, that would be difficult to put all in one place. Below, we have provided a timeline of the key events that happened on both sides of the world, highlighting the true global nature of the conflict.

1939

  • September 1st:, Germany invades Poland, starting World War Two.
  • September 3rd: Britain and France declare war on Germany.

1940

  • May 10th:Germany invades France, Belgium and the Netherlands.
  • May 26th: The evacuation of the Allied troops from Dunkirk begins.

1941

  • June 22nd: Operation Barbarossa begins, in which Germany invades the Soviet Union.
  • December 7th: Japan attacks Pearl Harbour, forcing the United States to enter the war.

1942

  • February 15th: British military forces in Singapore surrender to Japan, starting the Imperial Japanese occupation of Singapore.
  • June 4th-7th: The Battle of Midway in the Pacific between the US and Japan, ending in an American victory.
  • July and October-November:The first and second Battles of El Alamein result in an Allied victory and the Axis powers being pushed back.

1943

  • February 2nd:Axis powers surrender in Stalingrad in one of the most catastrophic battles of the war. This marks Germany's first major defeat.
  • September:The Allies invade Italy in a 2-week campaign, starting with an amphibious campaign, resulting in an Allied victory when Italy surrenders.

1944

  • June 6th:The Allies land in Normandy, France, beginning the liberation of France and the future victory on the Western front.
  • August: After a week long battle, on 25th August, the Nazis' surrender and Paris is liberated, with General Charles de Gaulle acting as leader of the provisional government.

1945

  • May 7th: Nazi Germany forces surrender in the West, and in the East on May 9th. Victory in Europe Day (VE Day) is declared for May 8th.
  • August 6th and 9th: The US drops atomic bombs in Hiroshima and Nagasaki, Japan. This is the first time nuclear weapons have been used on this scale in any conflict.
  • August 14th:Japan surrenders, and World War Two comes to an end.
